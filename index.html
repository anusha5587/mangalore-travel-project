Why Mangalore?

Mangalore, where the azure waves embrace a symphony of culture, history, and coastal allure

Mangalore, or Mangaluru, nestled on Karnataka's west coast along the Arabian Sea, is a captivating blend of tradition and progress. Steeped in history as a vital spice trading center, the city has evolved into a diverse economic powerhouse with flourishing industries like petrochemicals and information technology. Its tropical climate and rich cultural tapestry, boasting festivals like Mangalore Dasara, add vibrancy to the city.

Amidst the confluence of the Netravati and Gurupura rivers, Mangalore stands as an educational hub, home to institutions like Mangalore University and NITK. The city's religious harmony is reflected in its temples, churches, and mosques. Culinary enthusiasts are drawn to Mangalorean specialties like spicy seafood delicacies. With pristine beaches, historic landmarks, and a dynamic atmosphere, Mangalore remains a unique and enchanting destination in the heart of southern India.

Cafés in Mangalore

Pabbas Ideal Cafe

Pabbas Ideal Ice Cream Café, popularly known as Pabbas, is a beloved ice cream parlor in Mangalore, Karnataka, with a history dating back to 1974. Renowned for its delicious and diverse ice cream flavors, Pabbas has become an iconic destination for locals and tourists alike. The café offers a welcoming ambiance, making it a favorite spot for families and friends to indulge in sweet treats.

Address:

Bejai Main Rd, Lalbagh, Mangaluru, Karnataka 575003, India

What I like about it

Its wide variety of delicious and high-quality ice creams. They offer a diverse range of flavors, catering to different tastes and preferences. Some of the popular flavors include Gadbad (a unique ice cream sundae), Vanilla, Chocolate, Strawberry, Mango, and various seasonal offerings.

Brio Cafe & Grill

Brio is a café and grill located in Mangalore right next door to St. Aloysius College. It has a laid back, rustic industrial vibe to it with locally sourced murals on the walls. Think Edison bulbs, comfortable couches & funky metal chairs. They are all about good coffee! The cafe source's the best quality coffee beans that are roasted with love and care filling each cup with oodles of aroma and flavour.

Address:

Aloysius College Road Sainik Bhavan, First Floor, Mangalore 575001 India

What I like about it

When it comes to their food, they exclusively use the freshest local ingredients, whether it's buns or any meat or desserts . With an impressive menu featuring roasts and grilled foods bursting with punchy flavors, coupled with a wonderful ambiance, they offer a delightful dining experience.

The Liquid Lounge

Liquid Lounge, nestled on MG Road in Mangalore, is a chic coffee haven with a welcoming atmosphere. Renowned for its meticulously crafted coffee, this trendy spot offers a diverse menu of aromatic brews and delectable snacks.The skilled baristas at Liquid Lounge take pride in their artistry, ensuring that each cup is a delightful experience.
