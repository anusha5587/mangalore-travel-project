Why Mangalore?

Mangalore, where the azure waves embrace a symphony of culture, history, and coastal allure

Mangalore, officially known as Mangaluru, is a port city in the state of Karnataka, India. It is located on the west coast of the country, along the Arabian Sea.

Mangalore is a blend of tradition and modernity, and it holds significance both as a commercial hub and a culturally diverse city in the southern part of India.
